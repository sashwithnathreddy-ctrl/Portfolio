About PKL

The Pro Kabaddi League (PKL) is a premier franchise-based kabaddi league in India, launched in 2014 by Mashal Sports, which revolutionized the traditional sport into a high-intensity, televised spectacle. With 12 teams, it is one of India's most popular sports leagues, featuring top international and domestic talent. Patna Pirates are the most successful team with three titles, while Haryana Steelers are the defending champions (Season 11)
